CRYPTOCURRENCY AND ITS IMPACT

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency that operates on blockchain technology, ensuring secure and decentralized transactions without the need for banks or central authorities. Popular cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH) have revolutionized the financial industry by enabling fast, low-cost, and borderless payments.


While crypto provides financial inclusion and investment opportunities, it also comes with challenges such as price volatility, regulatory uncertainty, security risks, and environmental concerns due to high energy consumption. Despite these challenges, cryptocurrency continues to shape the future of digital finance and innovation.
